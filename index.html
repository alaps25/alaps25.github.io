We realized that there is a great disconnect between the real and the virtual for security solutions.
Real vs Virtual
Lounge room
The idea was to bridge this gap by using upcoming technologies. We start with a BIM file to understand the building structures and spaces, giving us enough idea about the doors and security requirements of the building.
This will enable the intelligence of understanding relationship of spaces with the security devices and people using it. Everything including doors, spaces, people become interactive and can be controlled directly.
Following things become possible now
- Click a space and see the occupants and a timelapse of the activity/heatmap
- Click a door or a camera to instantly control it.
- Pick a camera and see what all area it can surveil and where are the blind spots.
- Use depth sensors to record in 3D and also playback in 3D.
- Drag and drop a person into an area and the system will intelligently figure out the doors he/she needs access to.
- Tag a person and track is activity throughout the building.
